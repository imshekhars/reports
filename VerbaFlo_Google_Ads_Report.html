Key Narrative — What the Data Tells Us
Strategic interpretation of the 6-month Google Ads performance
📈 Scale achieved

Spend scaled 7.6× from Nov 2025 (£899) to Mar 2026 (£6,827) — showing strong commitment to growth. Brand impression share held strong at 24–28% CTR throughout, confirming growing brand awareness in target markets.

🌍 Market expansion

Started with UK only. Scaled to US from Dec 2025 (£486), added UAE in Feb 2026 (£374), maintained India brand presence (£25→£181). 4 active markets within 4 months of launch.

⚡ Feb impression spike

February 2026 impressions jumped to 357,502 — a 515% increase — driven by PMax launch (308k impressions) and Elise AI competitor campaign expansion. Demonstrates the platform's ability to scale reach rapidly when needed.

🔧 Structural improvements in Apr

April reflects deliberate restructuring — UK/US brand split, keyword pruning, pausing zero-ROI campaigns. Lower spend and volume in April is strategic, not decline. Account is being rebuilt for efficiency heading into May.
