Highlights
Key Trends — Google Ads
Brand campaign CTR held at 24–28% throughout all 6 months — one of the strongest signals of genuine brand demand and awareness growth in the UK market.
Paid Search sessions on GA4 grew from 864 (Nov) to 1,981 (Mar) — a 129% increase driven by expanding campaign coverage across brand, competitor and non-brand.
Market expansion went from UK-only in November to 4 active markets (UK, US, UAE, India) by February — 4 markets within 3 months of launch.
India Brand campaign consistently delivered the lowest CPC in the account — averaging £0.45–0.85 across all months, proving strong brand search efficiency.
Total impressions scaled from 15,986 (Nov) to a peak of 357,502 (Feb) — a 22× increase driven by PMax and Elise AI competitor campaign expansion.
Spend scaled 7.6× from £899 in November to £6,827 in March, showing accelerating investment confidence as the account matured and proved its performance.

Highlights
Key Trends — Meta Ads
Instant Form emerged as the winning format — generating 119 of 137 total leads (87%). UK Instant Form delivered £38.80 CPL in March — the most efficient lead month.
Total leads scaled 6.4× — from 8 leads in January to 51 in April — demonstrating consistent month-on-month growth despite rising CPM pressure.
UAE launched in April 2026 and immediately delivered 12 leads at £40.37 CPL — second-lowest CPL in the account — signalling a highly responsive new market.
Total reach grew from 12,514 unique users (Jan) to 190,093 (Mar) — a 15× increase in 3 months driven by campaign scaling and Advantage+ audience expansion.
Remarketing was successfully tested in March generating 4 leads — validating the strategy of re-engaging website visitors and Instagram engagers with conversion campaigns.
Industry event awareness campaigns (RETCON, Interface Student Housing) delivered CPMs as low as £1.8 — highly cost-efficient niche brand touchpoints.

Highlights
Key Trends — LinkedIn
Engagements grew from 361 (Dec) to 3,138 (Mar) — a 9× increase in LinkedIn post and ad interactions, building a progressively warmer audience pool.
Total clicks scaled from 358 (Dec) to 1,061 (Mar) — nearly 3× growth — driven by Sponsored Update campaigns running across UK, US and UAE simultaneously.
Message open rates reached 52–54% in Feb and Mar — significantly above the LinkedIn industry average of 35% — confirming precision targeting of the right personas.
Spend scaled from $434 (Dec) to $1,751 (Feb) — a 4× increase in 2 months — demonstrating growing confidence in LinkedIn as a brand-building channel.
ICP Company List targeting introduced in April generated the first LinkedIn conversion from a direct company-targeted approach — validating the ABM strategy.
Event-targeted campaigns (RETCON Austin, RETCON LA) reached industry-specific audiences at relevant property technology events — niche brand positioning at scale.
