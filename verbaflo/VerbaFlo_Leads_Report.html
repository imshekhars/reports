Key Observations
Trends and findings from the Jan–Apr lead pipeline
March had a 100% ICP rate — all 23 leads that month were ICP qualified, predominantly from UK Meta campaigns targeting BTR and PBSA operators. This is the highest quality lead month despite not being the highest volume month.
Google Ads delivered the strongest demo conversion — 16 Google Ads leads resulted in 11 demos booked (69% booking rate) and 7 demos done across all months, making it the most efficient channel for bottom-of-funnel conversion.
Organic Search is the highest quality channel — 10 organic leads generated 8 demo bookings (80% booking rate) and 5 demos completed. Every organic lead was ICP or near-ICP, confirming SEO traffic has the strongest intent in the funnel.
April's low demo rate reflects volume scaling — 65 leads in April but only 1 demo done. The majority are from broad Meta audiences still being qualified. 13 are marked "Yet to check" — once processed, the April conversion picture will improve.
